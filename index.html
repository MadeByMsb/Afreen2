About Us

AFREEN BUILDING MATERIALS TRADING CO. L.L.C (ABMTC)

ESTABLISHED IN 2006

Afreen Building Materials Trading Co. L.L.C (ABMTC) Established in 2006 headquartered in Abu Dhabi and 2 branches in Abu Dhabi, U.A.E. — is one of the stockiest of Paints, Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Civil Construction, and Oil Field Products. A centralized distribution system, along with fully computerized operations ensures stringent quality checks and superior standards are maintained for delivery times within the schedules provided. ABMTC operates from a full-fledged showroom in Abu Dhabi, Mussafah -M/9, M/14 and SHAHAMA with branches and extensive warehousing facilities. ABMTC is constantly striving to add quality products at affordable prices.

ABMTC is striving to ensure total customer satisfaction by providing dedicated service to all levels. We have a team of highly qualified sales professionals who are fully trained to be of service on all strategic business units. ABMTC has been associated with world-class Paint Manufacturer, Mechanical, Electrical Civil Construction, and oil field Products. We believe our services have immensely contributed to the infrastructure development requirements of the country for the past 16 years. Most of the products can be immediately supplied from our ready stock at short notice.
